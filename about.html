Who we are

"Marketing and privacy are not opposing forces — they are the same conversation about trust, told from different seats."

Data Pilots is building a trust platform for business leaders — giving deeper visibility into how their websites and tools handle client data, with clear next steps they can act on early. We exist to close the gap between growth and compliance, because when they work together from the start, businesses grow faster and build stronger client relationships.

When they don't, growth quietly costs trust — usually in places no one is watching, starting with the website. That's where we start too.

Our Co-Founders

The team behind it

Two people who've spent their careers on both sides of the trust problem — and built Data Pilots to close the gap between them.

Co-Founder & CEO
Antonia "Toni" Dumas
JD · CIPP/E · Former Big 4 Advisor

Toni has spent her career on both sides of the problem Data Pilots solves. She started in marketing and business — building brands, websites, campaigns, and representing global brands — across technology, beverage, fashion and beauty, and sports. Her path moved between marketing and law, beginning in consumer protection, before she returned to law school and focused her legal career on privacy and security.

A DC-licensed attorney, CIPP/E-certified privacy professional, and former Big 4 advisor, Toni has worked internationally with businesses from early-stage founders to global organisations. She is also a federal and state bar CLE instructor and adjunct JD professor, training specialists and translating complex compliance into things non-expert teams can act on.

Co-Founder, COO & Products Lead
Kara Kelly
Privacy · Cyber · Risk Management

Kara is a tech-driven leader and legally trained privacy expert with over a decade of experience in privacy, cyber, and risk management across global organisations and multiple industries. She brings the technical depth, legal understanding, and operational execution that turn the company's trust-and-growth thesis into a product businesses can actually use.

Focused on ethical data practices and building trust, Kara leads product and privacy operations — bridging technical, legal, and real-world application. Her work has spanned multiple jurisdictions and industries, shaping programmes that operate at scale without losing sight of the people data is meant to protect.
