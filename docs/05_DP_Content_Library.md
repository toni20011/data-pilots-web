# 05 — Content Library

## What it is

The **Resource Hub** is a library of practical guides written specifically for online training businesses. It's part of the free Community membership.

**Purpose:** Give members the knowledge to understand their findings and take action, even before they access Trust Panel. The resource hub is not generic compliance guidance — it's written for L&D business owners who are not legal or technical experts.

---

## Role in the product

The resource hub serves two functions:

1. **Value delivery** — gives community members something tangible immediately (not just "save your Snapshot results")
2. **Conversion support** — guides that explain specific risk areas create informed buyers for the Trust Panel

---

## Content principles

- Written for a business owner, not a lawyer or developer
- Plain language throughout — compliance references provided as context, not primary framing
- Action-oriented — every piece should tell the reader what to do, not just what's wrong
- L&D specific — examples and context drawn from the online training industry (LMS data, learner tracking, corporate procurement)

## Notes

The content library is also a long-term SEO and thought leadership asset. Guides that explain "what GDPR means for your LMS" or "how to pass an enterprise procurement review" are exactly the searches that bring in high-intent visitors who are already experiencing the problem Data Pilots solves.

Future consideration: whether guides are gated (community login required) or public (for SEO). A hybrid approach — public summaries with full guides behind a free community signup — is consistent with the acquisition model.
