# 00 — Data Pilots Overview

## What we are

Data Pilots is a trust and compliance checking product for **online training (L&D) businesses selling to corporate clients**.

We help them see what enterprise buyers check when they assess a supplier — and fix the gaps before those gaps cost a deal.

---

## The one thing to remember

> **"You built something worth trusting. Data Pilots gives you the full picture — and your team the path to act."**

This is the central positioning line. It lives in the announce bar, the Why Data Pilots section, and the About page thesis. The phrase **"your team"** is deliberate — it counters the assumption that "Data Pilots" + "AI" + "data" means a fully managed, done-for-you service.

---

## The problem we solve

Online training businesses lose corporate deals silently. A buyer googles them, finds:
- No privacy policy (or one that looks stale)
- No consent banner — or one that doesn't actually work
- Security headers missing
- No mention of how AI is used in the product

They don't say anything. They just move on.

The business owner never knows why the deal went quiet.

---

## What we build

### Free Trust Snapshot
A 5-minute scan of a public website URL. No account required. Returns:
- A trust score across 4 Trust Pillars (Transparency, Safety, UX, AI Trust)
- Findings with plain-language explanations and fix guides
- A preview of what buyers and auditors see

The Snapshot is the acquisition engine. It converts curiosity into a clear picture of what needs fixing.

### Trust Panel _(paid product — early access open)_
The working hub for closing trust gaps. One place to:
- See everything across 6 instruments (deeper than the Snapshot)
- Assign findings to the right team members and tools
- Track progress until gaps are closed
- Build a compliance posture you can share with buyers

The Panel is for businesses that have seen their Snapshot and want to act — especially those with enterprise deals on the line.

### Community
Free membership. The bridge between Snapshot and Panel. Members get:
- Saved Snapshot results, revisit any time
- Resource hub — practical guides written for L&D businesses
- Founding member rates on Trust Panel at launch
- Direct input into what gets built

## Who we serve

**Primary audience:** Online training businesses (solo L&D practitioners to small L&D companies) selling B2B — specifically into corporate/enterprise HR and procurement buyers.

**Their situation:**
- They're good at what they do (learning design, content, delivery)
- They're not data privacy experts
- They're losing deals to trust gaps they don't know about
- They don't have a legal team or a developer — they need plain-language guidance

**The buyer who checks them:**
- Enterprise HR buyer — cares about GDPR, learner data, supplier trust signals
- IT/security — checks headers, security posture
- Legal/procurement — wants DPAs, terms, policy coverage

---

## Two-level goals

**Have to meet (procurement/compliance):**
- Win corporate clients
- Pass procurement due diligence
- Meet compliance requirements (GDPR, CCPA, sector-specific)

**Want to reach (mission/impact):**
- Help good L&D businesses develop better leaders
- Get the right training to the organisations that need it
- Stop good work being blocked by invisible trust gaps

The line connecting them: _"Data Pilots doesn't just help you win deals. It helps more of your work reach the world."_
