US (CCPA)Global (GDPR)

What we check
  • Whether you have a current, readable privacy policy — not a copy-paste template
  • Cookie consent — does it exist, and does it actually work when someone opts out?
  • How clearly you describe what data you collect and why
  • Whether your terms of service cover your actual services
Why buyers care

Corporate L&D buyers look for your privacy policy on day one — and for training providers specifically, they want to know how you handle learner data. If it's missing, out of date, or silent on your LMS, they flag it before they've finished reading your proposal.

Certifications (ISO 27001)Audit (SOC 2)

What we check
  • Security headers — the invisible settings that tell browsers your site is safe (CSP, HSTS)
  • Whether your entire site runs over HTTPS — no exceptions
  • Whether your cookie banner actually stops tracking when someone clicks "Reject All"
  • Server configuration issues that automated IT scans pick up immediately
Why buyers care

Corporate IT teams run automated scans on every supplier's website. Failing basic checks gets you disqualified before the business team has had a chance to vouch for you. You may never even know it happened.

What's included

One place. Full control.
Proof you can share.

Trust Panel does what every other tool skips — it coordinates. The right people, tools, and AI on the right tasks, so gaps get closed, progress gets tracked, and you're never caught off guard by what a buyer finds.

Full cookie and consent audit
Every cookie named, categorised, and mapped against your consent setup — including what fires before consent, and what your banner actually does versus what it says.
Third-party tracker inventory
A full list of every tracker loading on your site, which jurisdiction laws apply to each one, and what your disclosure needs to say to stay clean.
Privacy policy completeness review
We map your actual tool stack against your policy — checking that what you use is what you've disclosed, in the language that matters for your specific client jurisdictions.
Data transfer mapping
Where your data goes, which countries it crosses, and whether your current contracts and SCCs cover the transfers your tools are actually making.
AI governance audit
Which AI tools you use, what data they can access, what your enterprise-tier settings actually do, and what your site needs to say to satisfy procurement questions before you're asked.
Fix guides for three audiences
Every finding comes with plain-language instructions for you as the owner, technical steps for your developer, and legal context for your compliance adviser. One roadmap, no translation needed.
Trust proof PDF
An exportable document showing your current trust score, what was checked, and your progress over time. Built to attach to proposals, RFPs, and procurement questionnaires.
Monthly re-scans and dashboard
Your score updates every month. You see what improved, what slipped, and what needs attention next — so trust stays green between client conversations, not just when you remember to check.
Full AI agent consent walkthrough
Hub exclusive
An AI agent visits your site and simulates the complete user consent journey — clicking, rejecting, and navigating exactly as a real visitor would across different jurisdictions. You get a step-by-step trace of what loads, what fires before consent, what your banner actually does versus what it says, and what visitors are really agreeing to. The level of detail your legal and compliance team needs — and that no manual check can replicate.
Procurement readiness check
For those working with enterprise and government clients: a gap analysis against the questions vendor security questionnaires actually ask, before procurement finds the gaps themselves.
Evidence archive and expert sharing
Every walkthrough generates a timestamped evidence record — screenshots, consent trace, and findings — stored in your Hub. Share directly with your legal counsel, DPO, or compliance adviser. Useful for internal reviews, regulatory queries, and demonstrating due diligence without starting from scratch each time.
Right fit

Built for online training businesses
selling to corporate clients

Trust Panel is for training businesses, coaches, and e-learning providers that deliver online and sell to corporate clients — and are ready to stop losing deals to invisible trust gaps.

This is built for you if…
You're the founder or owner of an online training business, coaching practice, or e-learning platform — and you're the one accountable for winning and keeping corporate clients
You're fielding trust and compliance questions from buyers — without a legal or IT team behind you
You've lost a deal — or nearly lost one — and couldn't fully explain why the buyer went quiet
You're coordinating a developer, a legal advisor, and a stack of tools — with no central view of your trust picture
You use a mix of tools — LMS, CRM, AI, email — and aren't confident what your obligations are across all of them
You want to be in control of where you stand — not dependent on others to tell you
This probably isn't for you if…
You have an in-house legal or compliance team already running regular checks — you don't need the central view, you have the team
You only sell to individual consumers — no corporate or enterprise clients in your pipeline
You want a fully managed service where someone else handles everything — Trust Panel makes it far easier to delegate confidently, but works best when you're the one directing
You haven't run your free Trust Snapshot yet — start there first
